## Perencanaan Seragam Safety Proyek

### Langkah 1: Risk Assessment

Sebelum menentukan kebutuhan APD, lakukan:

**Site Hazard Analysis:**
- Identifikasi semua hazard di site
- Kategorikan berdasarkan severity dan probability
- Tentukan control measures
- Tentukan APD yang diperlukan

**Task-Based Assessment:**
- List semua tugas di proyek
- Identifikasi hazard per tugas
- Tentukan APD spesifik per tugas
- Document dalam job safety analysis (JSA)

### Langkah 2: Penentuan Spesifikasi

Berdasarkan risk assessment, tentukan:

**Jenis APD per Posisi:**

| Posisi | APD Minimum | APD Tambahan |
|--------|-------------|--------------|
| General Worker | Helm, Safety Shoes, Vest | Gloves, Glasses |
| Scaffolder | + Full Body Harness | - |
| Welder | + FR Clothing | Welding Helmet, Gloves |
| Electrician | + FR Clothing | Insulated Gloves |
| Supervisor | + Radio | Tablet/Plans |

**Spesifikasi Teknis:**
- Bahan material
- Standar sertifikasi
- Warna dan identifikasi
- Fitur khusus

### Langkah 3: Perhitungan Quantity

**Faktor yang Mempengaruhi:**
- Jumlah pekerja per shift
- Jumlah shift per hari
- Turnover rate
- Visitor frequency
- Replacement factor (10-15% buffer)

**Formula:**
```
Total Sets = (Workers × Shifts) + Buffer + Visitors + Emergency Stock
```

### Langkah 4: Budgeting

**Komponen Budget:**

| Item | Persentase | Keterangan |
|------|-----------|------------|
| Initial Purchase | 60% | Pembelian awal |
| Replacement | 20% | Penggantian rusak/hilang |
| Contingency | 10% | Unexpected needs |
| Training | 5% | Training pemakaian |
| Admin | 5% | Management costs |

**Budget per Pekerja:**
- Basic PPE set: Rp 800.000 - 1.500.000
- Premium/specialized PPE: Rp 2.000.000 - 5.000.000
- Annual replacement: 20-30% dari initial

## Monitoring dan Maintenance

### Inspection Schedule

**Daily:**
- Visual check sebelum masuk site
- Report damage
- Immediate replacement jika rusak

**Weekly:**
- Supervisor inspection
- Document findings
- Plan replacements

**Monthly:**
- Comprehensive audit
- Inventory check
- Budget review

### Maintenance Program

**Cleaning:**
- Regular cleaning schedule
- Proper washing methods
- Drying procedures
- Storage

**Repair:**
- Minor repairs (if appropriate)
- Professional repair untuk critical items
- Replace rather than repair jika doubtful

**Replacement Criteria:**
- Physical damage
- Wear beyond serviceable
- Contamination
- Expiration date (jika ada)
- After incident exposure

## Tantangan Umum dan Solusi

### Tantangan 1: Budget Constraints

**Problem:** Budget terbatas tapi kebutuhan banyak

**Solutions:**
- Prioritize based on risk
- Phase implementation
- Negotiate payment terms
- Consider rental untuk short-term
- Buy quality (last longer)

### Tantangan 2: Worker Compliance

**Problem:** Pekerja tidak mau memakai APD

**Solutions:**
- Better training dan awareness
- More comfortable PPE
- Involve workers in selection
- Strict enforcement
- Lead by example

### Tantangan 3: High Turnover

**Problem:** Pekerja datang-pergi, APD hilang

**Solutions:**
- Robust sign-out system
- Deposit system
- Standard sizing
- Quick replacement process
- Clear policies

### Tantangan 4: Multiple Contractors

**Problem:** Setiap contractor punya standar berbeda

**Solutions:**
- Unified site standards
- Provide site PPE jika needed
- Clear specifications di contract
- Regular coordination meetings
- Consistent enforcement

## Tips untuk Project Manager

### 1. Start Early
- Include PPE in initial planning
- Budget dari awal
- Order dengan lead time yang cukup

### 2. Buy Quality
- Cheap PPE often costs more long-term
- Durability matters
- Worker comfort affects compliance

### 3. Involve Workers
- Get input on comfort dan usability
- Workers more likely to use PPE they helped select
- Build ownership

### 4. Lead by Example
- Always wear your own PPE
- Never shortcut safety
- Visible commitment

### 5. Make It Easy
- Easy access to PPE
- Quick replacement process
- Clear procedures

### 6. Document Everything
- Keep records of all PPE issued
- Track inspections
- Document incidents dan near-misses
