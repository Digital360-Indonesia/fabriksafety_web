## Teknik Aplikasi Logo

### 1. Bordir (Embroidery)

**Proses:**
Logo dijahit langsung ke kain menggunakan mesin bordir komputer.

**Kelebihan:**
- **Premium look** dan feel
- **Tahan lama** - tidak mudah pudar
- **Professional appearance**
- **Tekstur 3D** yang distinctive
- Cocok untuk berbagai bahan

**Kekurangan:**
- Biaya lebih tinggi untuk design complex
- Size terbatas - tidak cocok untuk logo sangat besar
- Waktu produksi lebih lama
- Tidak ideal untuk detail sangat halus

**Best untuk:**
- Logo perusahaan
- Nama employee
- Small to medium designs
- Long-term use

### 2. Sablon (Screen Printing)

**Proses:**
Tinta diaplikasikan ke kain melalui screen/stencil.

**Kelebihan:**
- **Cost-effective** untuk quantity besar
- **Warna solid** dan vibrant
- **Cepat** untuk production besar
- Fleksibel untuk berbagai size

**Kekurangan:**
- Bisa **crack** atau peel seiring waktu
- **Less breathable** di area printed
- Tidak se-formal bordir
- Durabilitas lebih rendah

**Best untuk:**
- Large logos
- Simple designs
- High volume orders
- Budget-conscious

### 3. DTF (Direct to Film)

**Proses:**
Desain di-print ke film khusus, lalu di-heat transfer ke kain.

**Kelebihan:**
- **Detail tinggi** termasuk gradient
- **Full color** tanpa batasan
- **Soft feel** pada kain
- Cepat untuk small orders

**Kekurangan:**
- Durabilitas medium
- Cost per piece relatif tinggi
- Quality bervariasi

**Best untuk:**
- Complex designs
- Small quantities
- Photo-quality logos

### 4. Heat Transfer

**Proses:**
Vinyl atau transfer paper di-press ke kain dengan panas.

**Kelebihan:**
- **Fast turnaround**
- Good untuk **names dan numbers**
- **Affordable** untuk small batches
- Variety of colors

**Kekurangan:**
- Less durable than embroidery
- Can peel over time
- Not breathable
- Less premium look

**Best untuk:**
- Individual names
- Small batches
- Variable data
- Quick turnaround needs

### 5. Patch (Lencana)

**Proses:**
Logo di-bordir terpisah sebagai patch, lalu dijahit ke seragam.

**Kelebihan:**
- **Very durable**
- **High detail** possible
- Dapat **dipindahkan** ke seragam baru
- Consistent quality

**Kekurangan:**
- Additional visible border
- Bulkier feel
- Higher cost
- Extra sewing required

**Best untuk:**
- Military/police style
- High-detail logos
- Collectible items
- Premium applications

## Memilih Teknik yang Tepat

### Faktor Pertimbangan

**1. Budget**
- Bordir: Medium-High
- Sablon: Low-Medium
- DTF: Medium
- Heat Transfer: Low
- Patch: High

**2. Quantity**
- High volume (>500): Sablon paling ekonomis
- Medium volume (100-500): Bordir atau Sablon
- Small volume (<100): Heat Transfer atau DTF

**3. Design Complexity**
- Simple logo: Semua teknik cocok
- Moderate detail: Bordir atau DTF
- High detail/foto: DTF atau Patch
- Text only: Semua teknik cocok

**4. Durability Needs**
- Long-term (>2 tahun): Bordir atau Patch
- Medium-term (1-2 tahun): DTF
- Short-term (<1 tahun): Heat Transfer

**5. Fabric Type**
- Thick fabric (drill, canvas): Semua teknik
- Thin fabric: Avoid heavy embroidery
- Waterproof: Avoid perforating techniques

## Desain Logo untuk Bordir

### Spesifikasi Ideal

**Size:**
- **Chest logo:** 8-12 cm width
- **Back logo:** 20-30 cm width
- **Sleeve logo:** 6-8 cm width
- **Collar:** 4-6 cm width

**Complexity:**
- Simplifikasi design untuk bordir
- Minimal line weight: 1mm
- Avoid gradients (convert to solid colors)
- Limit colors (3-6 ideal)

**Format File:**
- Vector format (AI, EPS, SVG) preferred
- High-resolution PNG jika vector tidak available
- Actual size specification
- Color reference (Pantone codes)

### Design Tips

**1. Simplify**
- Hilangkan unnecessary details
- Gunakan solid shapes
- Limit fine lines
- Consider from distance visibility

**2. Color Selection**
- Pertimbangkan warna seragam
- Enough contrast untuk visibility
- Brand colors consistency
- Limit to 3-6 thread colors

**3. Placement**
- **Left chest:** Standard untuk logo utama
- **Right chest:** Nama employee atau department
- **Back:** Logo besar atau company name
- **Sleeve:** Secondary logo atau certification marks
- **Collar:** Small brand identifier

**4. Scalability**
- Design should work at various sizes
- Test readability at actual size
- Consider minimum size requirements

## Estimasi Biaya

### Faktor yang Mempengaruhi Harga

**1. Stitch Count**
Semakin banyak jahitan, semakin mahal.
- Simple logo: 5,000-10,000 stitches
- Moderate logo: 10,000-20,000 stitches
- Complex logo: 20,000+ stitches

**2. Size**
Larger logo = more stitches = higher cost

**3. Colors**
Setiap warna tambahan meningkatkan cost
- 1-3 colors: Base price
- 4-6 colors: +20-30%
- 7+ colors: +40-50%

**4. Quantity**
Higher volume = lower unit cost
- <50 pieces: Highest per unit
- 50-200 pieces: Medium discount
- 200+ pieces: Volume discount

**5. Placement**
Multiple placements add cost
- Single location: Base
- Two locations: +50-70%
- Three+ locations: +100%+

## Maintenance

### Perawatan Seragam Bordir

**Washing:**
- Follow fabric care instructions
- Wash inside out untuk protect embroidery
- Use mild detergent
- Avoid bleach on embroidered areas
- Cool or warm water preferred

**Drying:**
- Air dry ideal
- If using dryer, low heat
- Do not overdry

**Ironing:**
- Iron inside out
- Place cloth over embroidery
- Medium heat
- Do not iron directly on embroidery

**Storage:**
- Hang atau fold properly
- Avoid crushing embroidery
- Clean before storage

### Lifespan

**Expected Durability:**
- Quality embroidery: 2-5 years with proper care
- Can outlast the garment itself
- Colors may fade slightly over time
- Threads remain intact with good care

## Common Problems dan Solutions

### 1. Puckering

**Problem:** Fabric bunches around embroidery

**Causes:**
- Wrong backing
- Too tight hooping
- Design too dense

**Solution:**
- Use appropriate backing
- Proper hooping technique
- Adjust design density

### 2. Thread Breaks

**Problem:** Threads break during wear/wash

**Causes:**
- Poor quality thread
- Incorrect tension
- Sharp edges in design

**Solution:**
- Quality thread
- Proper tension settings
- Smoothen design edges

### 3. Color Mismatch

**Problem:** Thread color doesn't match brand color

**Causes:**
- Inaccurate color specification
- Limited thread color options

**Solution:**
- Provide Pantone reference
- Request thread color approval
- Use custom-dyed threads if needed

### 4. Misalignment

**Problem:** Logo not straight atau centered

**Causes:**
- Poor hooping
- Inaccurate placement marking
- Machine issues

**Solution:**
- Precise marking
- Quality control
- Reputable vendor
