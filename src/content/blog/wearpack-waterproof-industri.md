## Memahami Rating Waterproof

### Water Resistance Levels

| Rating | Kemampuan | Aplikasi |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| **Water-repellent** | Menolak air ringan | Light drizzle |
| **Water-resistant** | Tahan air moderate | Short exposure |
| **Waterproof** | Tidak tembus air | Extended exposure |
| **Submersible** | Bisa dalam air | Underwater work |

### Waterproof Rating (mm/H2O)

| Rating | Level | Pemakaian |
|--------|-------|-----------|
| 1,000-5,000 mm | Light rain | Urban commute |
| 5,000-10,000 mm | Moderate rain | Outdoor work |
| 10,000-20,000 mm | Heavy rain | Heavy outdoor |
| 20,000+ mm | Extreme conditions | Maritime, extreme weather |

### Breathability Rating (g/m²/24hr)

| Rating | Level | Comfort |
|--------|-------|---------|
| <5,000 | Low | Bisa panas |
| 5,000-10,000 | Moderate | Balanced |
| 10,000-15,000 | Good | Comfortable |
| 15,000+ | Excellent | Very breathable |

## Teknologi Waterproof

### 1. Membrane Technology

| Teknologi | Cara Kerja | Kelebihan |
|-----------|------------|-----------|
| **Gore-Tex** | Microporous membrane | Premium, breathable |
| **eVent** | Direct venting | Excellent breathability |
| **Dermizax** | Smart membrane | Adapts to conditions |
| **Hyvent** | PU coating | Affordable alternative |
| **Patagonia H2No** | Proprietary | Eco-friendly options |

### 2. DWR (Durable Water Repellent)

**Fungsi:** Coating pada outer fabric yang membuat air mengalir (beading)

| Type | Durability | Eco-Factor |
|------|------------|------------|
| C6 DWR | Good | Better than C8 |
| C8 DWR | Excellent | Environmental concerns |
| C0 DWR | Moderate | Most eco-friendly |
| PFAS-free | Variable | Eco-friendly |

### 3. Seam Sealing

| Type | Description | Protection Level |
|------|-------------|------------------|
| Taped seams | Tape applied over seams | Full waterproof |
| Welded seams | Heat-bonded | Best waterproof |
| Critically taped | Only stress points taped | Moderate |
| Unsealed seams | No sealing | Water-resistant only |

### 4. Construction Methods

| Method | Description | Benefit |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| **2-Layer** | Fabric + membrane/lining | Affordable |
| **2.5-Layer** | Fabric + membrane + print | Lightweight |
| **3-Layer** | Fabric + membrane + liner | Durable, premium |

## Perawatan Waterproof Wearpack

### Washing Guidelines

| Step | Instruction |
|------|-------------|
| 1 | Check care label |
| 2 | Close all zippers |
| 3 | Use technical wash atau mild detergent |
| 4 | Avoid fabric softener |
| 5 | Wash in cold/warm water |
| 6 | Air dry atau low heat |
| 7 | Re-apply DWR if needed |

### DWR Re-application

**Tanda DWR Sudah Hilang:**
- Air tidak lagi beading
| Fabric menyerap air (wetting out)

**Cara Re-apply:**
1. Wash garment clean
2. Apply DWR spray atau wash-in
3. Heat activate (tumble dry low atau iron low)

### Storage

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Store clean and dry | Store wet |
| Hang atau fold loosely | Compress tightly |
| Cool, dry place | Direct sunlight |
| Air out occasionally | In plastic bags |

## Common Mistakes

### 1. Mengabaikan Breathability

**Masalah:** Wearpack waterproof tanpa breathability = sweat trap

**Solusi:** Pilih dengan breathability rating yang sesuai aktivitas

### 2. Tidak Memperhatikan Seam Sealing

**Masalah:** Air masuk melalui jahitan

**Solusi:** Pastikan fully taped atau welded seams

### 3. Salah Size

**Masalah:** Terlalu ketat = restricted movement; Terlalu longgar = water entry

**Solusi:** Ukur dengan layer yang akan dipakai di bawah

### 4. Tidak Merawat DWR

**Masalah:** DWR hilang, fabric wetting out

**Solusi:** Re-apply DWR secara berkala

### 5. Menyimpan dalam Kondisi Basah

**Masalah:** Mold, mildew, damage pada membrane

**Solusi:** Selalu keringkan sebelum simpan
